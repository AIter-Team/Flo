## **Quant**

**Domain:** Daily Financial Operations & Cash Flow

### **Description**

The Quant is the "Bookkeeper" of the system. It deals with the **present moment** and the **immediate past**. Its primary focus is ensuring accurate data entry for daily transactions and maintaining adherence to short-term budgets.

### **Core Responsibilities**

- **Transaction Logging:** Recording income and expenses in real-time.
    
- **Budget Creation:** Setting up monthly or category-specific budgets.
    
- **Budget Tracking:** Monitoring spending against defined limits (e.g., "You have used 80% of your dining budget").
    

### **Example Use Cases**

- "I just bought a coffee for $5." (Expense Logging)
    
- "I got my paycheck today." (Income Logging)
    
- "Create a budget for groceries." (Budgeting)
    
- "How much have I spent on transport this month?" (Expense Review)
    
- "Am I over budget?" (Status Check)
