## **Capitalist**

**Domain:** Net Worth, Assets & Liabilities

### **Description**

The Capitalist is the "Wealth Manager" of the system. It deals with the **Balance Sheet**. Its primary focus is maximizing the user's net worth by managing what the user _owns_ (Investments) and what the user _owes_ (Liabilities).

### **Core Responsibilities**

- **Investment Tracking:** Logging stocks, bonds, crypto, or real estate holdings.
    
- **Liability Management:** Tracking loans, credit card debt, mortgages, and subscriptions.
    
- **Net Worth Calculation:** Aggregating assets minus liabilities.
    
- **Wealth Defense:** Identifying high-interest debts or fees that erode wealth.
    

### **Example Use Cases**

- "I bought 5 shares of AAPL." (Investment Entry)
    
- "Add my new car loan to the system." (Debt Entry)
    
- "What is my current net worth?" (Wealth Analysis)
    
- "List all my current debts." (Liability Check)
    
- "I want to track my mortgage payments." (Long-term Debt)
