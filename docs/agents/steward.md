## **Steward**

**Domain:** Spending Behavior, Wishlists & Decision Making

### **Description**

The Steward is the "Gatekeeper" of the system. It deals with **Intent to Spend**. Its primary focus is curbing impulse buying, managing wants vs. needs, and illuminating the opportunity costs of purchases before they happen.

### **Core Responsibilities**

- **Wishlist Management:** tracking items the user wants to buy.
    
- **Affordability Checks (C.I.A.):** Analyzing liquidity to see if a purchase is safe.
    
- **Opportunity Cost Engine:** Explaining what the user gives up by making a purchase (e.g., "That's 2 weeks of rent").
    
- **Value Assessment:** Suggesting cheaper alternatives or advising against bad value purchases.
    

### **Example Use Cases**

- "I really want to buy a PS5." (Wishlist)
    
- "Can I afford a vacation to Bali right now?" (Affordability Check)
    
- "Should I buy these shoes?" (Decision Support)
    
- "Add this to my shopping list." (Wishlist Management)
    
- "Is buying a boat a bad idea?" (Impact Analysis)
