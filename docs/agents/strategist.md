## **Strategist**

**Domain:** Future Planning, Goals & Simulations

### **Description**

The Strategist is the "Planner" of the system. It deals with the **Future**. Its primary focus is bridging the gap between the user's current state and their desired future state through goal setting and simulation.

### **Core Responsibilities**

- **SMART Goals:** Creating Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound financial targets.
    
- **Automation Rules:** Creating behavioral triggers (e.g., "Save $5 every time I run").
    
- **Life Event Planning:** Estimating costs for weddings, babies, housing, or retirement.
    
- **Future Simulation:** Projecting net worth over 10-30 years and running "What If" scenarios (e.g., "What if I lose my job?").
    

### **Example Use Cases**

- "I want to save $10k for a wedding." (Goal Setting)
    
- "How much do I need to retire at 45?" (Life Event/Fire Calculation)
    
- "If I save $500 a month, what will I have in 10 years?" (Projection)
    
- "Create a rule to save money when I buy coffee." (Automation)
    
- "Will I be poor in the future?" (Simulation)
